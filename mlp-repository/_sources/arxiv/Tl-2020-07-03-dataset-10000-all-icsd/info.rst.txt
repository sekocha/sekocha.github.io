Tl-2020-07-03-dataset-10000-all-icsd
====================================================

.. image:: summary/pareto.png
   :height: 350px

The current structure dataset comprises 10,000 structures generated from unique ICSD prototype structures composed of single elements with zero oxidation state. A more detailed procedure is found in `Phys. Rev. B 99, 214108 (2019) <https://journals.aps.org/prb/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevB.99.214108>`_. The procedure to estimate interatomic potentials from the dataset is found in `Phys. Rev. B 99, 214108 (2019) <https://journals.aps.org/prb/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevB.99.214108>`_ and `Phys. Rev. B 102, 174104 (2020) <https://journals.aps.org/prb/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevB.102.174104>`_.


- **All Pareto optimal MLPs are available** :download:`here         <pot-Tl-2020-07-03-dataset-10000-all-icsd.tar.gz>`.
