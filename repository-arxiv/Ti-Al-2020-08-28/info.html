Ti-Al-2020-08-28¶

The current structure dataset comprises structures generated from binary ICSD prototype alloy structures. A more detailed procedure is found in Phys. Rev. B 102, 174104 (2020). The procedure to estimate interatomic potentials from the dataset is found in Phys. Rev. B 99, 214108 (2019) and Phys. Rev. B 102, 174104 (2020).
